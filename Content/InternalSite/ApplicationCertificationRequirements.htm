CompletEPA ACR 2015-12-14 This document presents Application Certification Requirements for Surescripts customers. The audience for this document includes the customer's development, business analyst, product managers and implementation staff, and representatives of the Surescripts’ integration staff. The Application Certification Requirements are a set of additional requirements above those mandated in the Surescripts CompletEPA® Implementation Guide and in the Surescripts CompletEPA® Accelerator Implementation Guide. All of the application certification requirements must be met in order for a customer to access the Surescripts’ production network. Published by: Megan Hayden

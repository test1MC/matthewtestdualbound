PMBC Message Flow

1). The provider vendor downloads Formulary data.

2). The provider vendor sends an Eligibility Request for a patient. Surescripts forwards the Eligibility Request to the PBM/payer.

3). The PBM/payer sends an Eligibility Response including coverage information for the patient. The Subscriber PBM Unique Member ID can be found in the 271 Eligibility Response from the PBM/payer in loop 2100C NM109. Surescripts forwards the message to the provider vendor.

4). Once the medication, days supply, quantity, quantity unit of measure and pharmacy have been selected, the provider vendor sends a PMBC Request for patient-specific estimated cost and benefit data.

5). The PBM/payer processes the request, formats a PMBC Response message with patient-specific estimated cost and benefit information and sends to Surescripts. Surescripts forwards the PMBC Response synchronously to the provider vendor.

6). Based on the information supplied, the provider vendor sends a NewRx to Surescripts. Surescripts forward the NewRx to the pharmacy.

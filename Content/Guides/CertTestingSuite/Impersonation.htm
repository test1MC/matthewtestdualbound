Impersonation

Go to Admin Console>Certification Suite. In the Certification & Testing Suite, all interaction is performed while impersonating an account and portal to which you have access.

The Certification Suite Main window begins with an Impersonation section as shown below.

The two drop-down boxes allow you to make an impersonated Account/Portal selection. After you’ve selected your values the page updates with the impersonated context. Test Case Execution or Profile Management makes use of the selected impersonation values for tracking and storage.

Using the Cert Suite

From the Admin Console, click the Certification Suite button in the left menu toolbar. The Certification & Testing Suite Home Main screen opens. This Main window is comprised of three primary sections: Impersonation, Administration and Test Case Search Filters. To navigate to various screens, you may use the quick access tool bar (described below) or click on the desired link in the Administration section.

Certification Toolbar

The Certification quick access toolbar is located at the top of each page in the Certification Suite. The icons in this toolbar allow quick access to the major sections in the suite.

If you hold your mouse over a button, a brief description of the icon’s action displays.

Additional information is provided to the right of the icons. This information displays throughout the suite to keep you informed of your current impersonation context in addition to providing extra information (in certain sections) when editing hierarchical information.

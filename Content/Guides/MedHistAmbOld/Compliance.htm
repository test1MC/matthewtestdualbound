Compliance

While implementing e-prescribing messaging, the customer is required to comply with the customer responsibilities itemized in the then current ‘Surescripts Network Operations Guide’ (NOG). These responsibilities are in the areas of certification requirements, production operations requirements, directory management, training, and support. Every customer is required to have a signed NOG acknowledgement form on file.

For more information please refer to the NOG which was distributed to your organization when you signed your contract.

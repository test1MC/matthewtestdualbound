About Surescripts

About This Guide

This Surescripts Medication History for Ambulatory Implementation Guide was created to assist Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBMs) and prescriber system vendors in developing and transferring messages needed to provide medication history to physicians in an ambulatory setting. This document represents XML and EDIFACT implementations.

The audience for this document includes PBMs responsible for the creation of responses for Medication History for Ambulatory, Medication History for Reconciliation, and/or Medication History Panel Management, and for prescriber system vendors for the creation of requests and understanding of responses.

Related Guides

In addition to this guide there is a guide for Medication History for Reconciliation in an acute setting, which is was created to assist hospitals and Long Term Care facilities in developing and transferring messages needed to provide physicians treating hospitalized or Long Term Care patients access to outpatient medication history.

Formulary Roll-Up Exception Reports

Exception reports show the PBM/payer data that needs to be reviewed due to inconsistent or obsolete data. An initial set of reports is automatically created upon the initial load and the reports are made available in the same location as the formulary response file. Once the initial set of reports is created, the reports will be generated quarterly or upon request. See the Surescripts Prescription Benefit Implementation Guide for more information on response files. Two sets of reports will always be available (current and the most recent prior to the current). The exception reports available are described below.

Missing or Obsolete NDCs

Surescripts will provide PBM/payers with a report that shows how many NDCs within the formulary files do not match or match to obsolete NDCs in the major drug databases. Those NDCs that cannot be found will not be included in the roll-up process since they cannot be identified. The NDCs that map to obsolete NDCs will be used for the roll-up, but will not be candidates for the Representative NDC being provided to the prescriber vendors. These NDCs should be investigated by the PBM/payers to verify their validity.

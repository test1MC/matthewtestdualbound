About this Guide

This Formulary Roll-up Supplement Guide was created to inform Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBM/payers) about the process Surescripts performs for roll-up of formulary information. This is a supplement to the Surescripts Prescription Benefit Implementation Guide. Formulary information is provided to Surescripts by all participant PBM/payers.

This data identifies formulary status, coverage, and copay data for the payers' active members. Today, most PBM/payers are providing formulary files at the NDC-11 packaged drug level. All prescriber vendors prescribe at a dispensable drug level (drug name, strength, and form). In order to display formulary correctly, the data must be rolled up to a dispensable drug level. This process involves Surescripts performing this roll-up feature before the data is supplied to the prescriber vendor. Surescripts determines a representative NDC and rolls all formulary information to this level.

During the roll-up process, Surescripts identifies discrepancies in the files provided. Surescripts generates reports that indicate conflicts in formulary status, coverage factors, and copay details. The reports will be made available to the PBM/payer in a .csv file for periodic download and review.

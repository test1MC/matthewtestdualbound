Service Availability

Service availability is defined as the ability to accept messages, transactions, or files. Surescripts availability management aims to sustain availability to support customers at a justifiable cost. The high-level activities performed to realize these availability requirements include: availability planning, availability monitoring, availability reporting, change management, and communication of maintenance activities.

Availability management addresses each product and service in production to perform at an agreed upon level over a specified period of time as measured over the twelve month period ending on the last day of the previous calendar month.

Availability = [(Total Time – Unplanned Downtime) /Total Time]

Availability is the long-term average fraction of time that the specified Surescripts product is in service and satisfactorily performing its intended function.

Surescripts Scheduled Outages & Maintenance

Surescripts may conduct maintenance online at any hour when a service interruption is not required. Planned outages will not exceed seven (7) hours per month. Surescripts makes a reasonable effort to conduct scheduled system maintenance that potentially requires a network outage during periods of lowest transaction volume. The established change window is Wednesday evenings from 10 PM to 2 AM ET, excluding Surescripts holidays.

Surescripts will notify customers four (4) business days prior to any planned or potential maintenance related outages outside of the established Wednesday night change window set forth above. Surescripts may provide advance notice by email, phone, fax, or website for significant maintenance events that have customer impact.

Surescripts Unscheduled Outages & Emergency Maintenance

Surescripts will make a commercially reasonable effort to provide 30 minutes advance notification of emergency maintenance or within 30 minutes of an unplanned outage. All notifications shall be sent to customer’s designated point of contact.

Customer Scheduled Outages & Maintenance

Customers may conduct maintenance online at any hour when a service interruption is not required. However, customer must make a reasonable effort to conduct scheduled system maintenance that requires an outage during periods of lowest transaction volume. Customer will notify Surescripts four (4) business days prior to any planned outages that may potentially impact transactions or file load responses to that customer. Customer may provide up to fourteen (14) days advance notice by email, phone or Surescripts Support Case for significant maintenance events that have material connectivity or processing impact (e.g. SSL connectivity changes, firewall updates, etc.).

Customer Unscheduled Outages & Emergency Maintenance

Customer will make a commercially reasonable effort to provide 30 minutes advance notification of emergency maintenance or within 30 minutes of an unplanned outage. All notifications shall be sent to Surescripts Customer Support via a support case.

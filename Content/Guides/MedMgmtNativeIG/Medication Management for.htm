Medication Management for Adherence Overview

With Surescripts Medication Management for Adherence, you’ll get actionable data at the point of care to increase adherence and improve patient outcomes while reducing costs.

Medication Management for Adherence puts patient-specific adherence summaries and real-time messages from PBMs in your EHR workflow. With this actionable intelligence, you can quickly identify issues with medication adherence and gaps in care. Then you can address those problems while patients are still in your office.

Prerequisites for Medication Management for Adherence

When there is a patient encounter, the provider vendor should run an eligibility request/response (X12 270 and 271 messages) to obtain the Payer Identification and the PBM Unique ID needed to identify the patient. If there is no patient encounter, the demographic MPI search is used. See section Demographic MPI Search for more information.

RDS Message Examples

The following scenarios all begin with the same ADT A01 message as a trigger. The responses are displayed for an RDS message format.

Scenario:

  1. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A. The patient history is comprised of two drugs.
  2. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A and PBM B. Patient history exists at both PBMs.
  3. Patient was not found at Surescripts.
  4. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A. No medication history was found at PBM A.
  5. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A. An error occurred while attempting to connect to PBM A.

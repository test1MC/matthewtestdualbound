Introduction

Document Purpose

This Surescripts Implementation Guide was created to assist hospitals in developing and transferring messages needed to provide physicians treating hospitalized patients access to outpatient medication history and assist Pharmaceutical Benefits Management (PBM) companies as a claims source in responding to a medication request and developing a medication response.

The audience for this document includes any Participant responsible for developing a system interface for these transactions. This guide describes the Surescripts Medication History – Acute transaction sets and provides other information needed for their implementation.

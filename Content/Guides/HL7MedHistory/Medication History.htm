Medication History

Introduction

Once a patient has been identified by Surescripts, Surescripts creates and submits a Medication History request (RXHREQ) to the appropriate source. The source sends back a response (RXHRES) to Surescripts to describe the patient’s medication history. If a Medication History response contains the maximum of 50 drugs, Surescripts will make additional requests until there are no more drugs available or an entire year’s worth of history is represented.

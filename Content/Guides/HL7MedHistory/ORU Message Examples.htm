ORU Message Examples

The following scenarios all begin with the same ADT A01 message as a trigger. The responses are displayed for an ORU message format.

Scenario:

  1. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A, B, and C. The patient history is comprised of multiple drugs.
  2. Patient was not found at Surescripts.
  3. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A. No medication history was found at PBM A.
  4. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A. An error occurred while attempting to connect to PBM A.
  5. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A. An error occurred while attempting to connect to PBM A.
  6. Patient was identified as a member of PBM A and also had fill history from Pharmacy A

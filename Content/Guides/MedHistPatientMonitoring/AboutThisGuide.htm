About This Guide

Surescripts created this document to assist Care Managers in implementing the Medication History for Patient Monitoring product, which delivers real-time medication alerts based on user-customized criteria directly into the provider's workflow.

The audience for this document includes Care Managers responsible for implementing Medication History for Patient Monitoring.

The terms "message" and "transaction" are used interchangeably throughout this guide. "Transaction" is used when referencing language pulled directly from a standards organization document which uses the term "transaction".

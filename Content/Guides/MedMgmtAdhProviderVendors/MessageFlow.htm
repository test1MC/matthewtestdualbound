Message Flow

Medication Management for Adherence provides provider vendors with the electronic delivery of PBM/payer medication adherence messages and patient medication adherence profile. Through the provider vendor interface, the provider can view and respond to PBM/payer medication adherence messages and access the patient's adherence profile. The graphic below depicts the transaction flow between customers in this process.

Figure #1 Surescripts Medication Management for Adherence Message Flow Diagram

  1. PBM/payer sends messages (High Risk Medication, Medication Adherence, and/or Missing Medication) and PDC ratios to Surescripts destined for a provider and a specific patient. Messages and PDCs are XML or JSON based, discrete and structured data elements.

  2. Surescripts processes messages and PDC ratios upon receiving, forwards urgent messages directly to the provider’s worklist.
  3. Non-urgent messages and PDC ratios are available during the patient visit.
  4. Surescripts closes out those messages which have been acted upon, ensuring provider’s worklist and patient’s messages list only display messages needing viewing and attention.
  5. There are two ways Surescripts delivers messages into a provider’s environment:

    1. When the PBM/payer designates a provider, urgent messages ( will be delivered to the provider’s worklist.

    2. All messages and PDC ratios will be available for viewing within patient’s chart.

    6. The PBM/payer is responsible for sending the NPI to Surescripts, which is then used to route the message to the appropriate provider SPI(s).

  6. Physicians can provide feedback for messages read and feedback is sent to Surescripts for processing.
  7. PBM/payer pulls feedback to complete the communication loop.

Medication Management for Adherence Overview

Medication Management for Adherence provides managed and interactive medication management information to providers during and in-between patient visits, in partnership with PBM/payer and provider vendor partners. The product will:

  • Start with a core set of messages, visible to providers that provide specific and actionable medication information for a patient.
  • For every message, Surescripts will receive specific data elements from PBM/payers. Content will be standardized across message type.
  • Enable providers to view Proportion of Days Covered (PDC) information at all times.
  • Intelligently manage the information and messages to prevent ‘alert fatigue’.
  • Create feedback for the PBM/payers in an automated and elective fashion.

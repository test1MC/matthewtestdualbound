PrescriberAgent Usage Examples

The following examples show the looping of the Prescriber element when reporting different physician entities. The PrescriberAgent element is used to denote the person entering data into the message and can be a non-prescriber or a covering prescriber. In the case of a covering prescriber, appropriate identifications should be included such as NPI or DEA. Supervisor information may be needed based upon state law. A supervisor is the supervising physician under whose authority the prescription is being prescribed by the primary care prescriber.

Denoting Only the Prescriber

<Prescriber>

   <NonVeterinarian>

       <Identification>

         <NPI>1234567890</NPI>

       </Identification>

       <Name>

         <LastName>JONES</LastName>

         <FirstName>MARK</FirstName>

       </Name>

        <Address>

           <AddressLine1>211 CENTRAL ROAD</AddressLine1>

           <City>JONESVILLE</City>

           <StateProvince>TN</StateProvince>

           <PostalCode>37777</PostalCode>

           <CountryCode>US</CountryCode>

        </Address>

        <CommunicationNumbers>

           <PrimaryTelephone>

             <Number>6152219800</Number>

           </PrimaryTelephone>

        </CommunicationNumbers>

    </NonVeterinarian>

</Prescriber>

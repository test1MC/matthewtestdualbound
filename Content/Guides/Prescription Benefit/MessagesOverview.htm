Prescription Benefit & Medication History Process Flow

The Surescripts Prescription Benefit & Medication History service provides physicians with the electronic delivery of PBM/payer member data in an ambulatory setting. Through the Physician System interface, the physician can request patient information such as eligibility and pharmacy benefit coverage, formulary information, and medication history.

Eligibility Transaction Flow

The following steps depict the Eligibility transaction flow:

  1. A requester sends an Eligibility request to Surescripts.
  2. Surescripts validates the format of the transaction.
  3. Surescripts locates the patient based on demographics information and uniquely identifies the patient.
  4. Surescripts determines to which PBM/payers the Eligibility request should be directed.
  5. The PBM/payer verifies the patient, responds with a 271 transaction indicating the patient’s eligibility status, and sends the 271 message back to Surescripts.
  6. Surescripts validates the format of the incoming 271, consolidates all 271 responses and sends the information back to the requester.

Product Overview

About Specialty Patient Onboard

The Specialty Patient Onboard (SPO) product gives providers that prescribe specialty medications the ability to electronically populate and submit enrollment data from within the provider vendor system workflow. The user completes an enrollment form via the SPO Accelerator. Surescripts compiles and sends the enrollment data to the appropriate specialty pharmacy for the enrollment of the patient. Enrollment forms can be based on a specific medication, a medical condition, or a medical specialty with a medication mapping to one or more of the enrollment forms. In addition, Surescripts will maintain a general enrollment form that can be used for any medication where a specific enrollment form has not been identified. Prior to submitting the enrollment data, the provider vendor system will send an SPO Medication Identification Service Request to determine whether or not to initiate the SPO process.

About This Guide

Surescripts created this document to assist provider vendors in implementing the SPO Accelerator. The audience for this document includes provider vendors integrating the SPO Accelerator within an existing provider vendor system.

The “SPO Accelerator” may also be referred to as “the Accelerator” throughout the rest of the guide.

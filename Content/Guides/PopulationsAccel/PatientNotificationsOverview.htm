Medication History for Populations Patient Notifications Overview

About Medication History for PopulationsPatient Notifications

The healthcare landscape is shifting to value-based care and ACOs, pushing risk further toward IDNs and hospitals more than ever before. As a result effective care management is essential to improve patient care and reduce costs.

Prescriptions are a critical tool for treating chronically ill patients, who account for 91% of all prescriptions in the U.S. Access to timely dispensed medication data is a prerequisite for effective care management of patient populations and is currently not available on a near real-time basis today.

Medication History for Populations Patient Notifications addresses this problem by delivering near real-time notifications based on user-customized criteria directly into the customer’s workflow (e.g., cardiology patient’s statin prescription was not picked at a pharmacy). Surescripts will generate these notifications via the Patient Notifications Accelerator by continuously monitoring the patient’s medication behaviors for customer-selected notification criteria. Allowing Surescripts to generate notifications based on continuous data monitoring will result in more insight vs. making a care provider request information themselves.

About This Guide

Surescripts created this document to assist provider vendors in implementing the Medication History for PopulationsPatient Notifications Accelerator product. The audience for this document includes any provider vendor responsible for developing a system interface for these electronic messages.

The “Medication History for PopulationsPatient Notifications Accelerator” may also be referred to as “the Accelerator” throughout the rest of the guide.

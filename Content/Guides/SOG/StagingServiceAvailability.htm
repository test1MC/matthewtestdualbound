Staging Service Availability

Service availability is defined as the ability to accept messages, transactions, or files. Surescripts availability management aims to sustain availability to support customers at a justifiable cost. The high-level activities performed to realize these availability requirements include: availability planning, availability monitoring, availability reporting, change management, and communication of maintenance activities.

Availability management addresses each product and service in staging to perform at an agreed upon level over a specified period of time as measured over the twelve month period ending on the last day of the previous calendar month.

Availability = [(Total Time – Unplanned Downtime) /Total Time]

Availability is the long-term average fraction of time that the specified Surescripts product is in service and satisfactorily performing its intended function.

Surescripts Staging Environment Availability

Surescripts Staging Environment is expected to be available from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM ET Monday through Friday, excluding Surescripts' holidays. Surescripts will send a Support Notification email to connected customers to notify them of a Surescripts observed holiday one week before the holiday. Surescripts will make an effort to limit service interruptions during staging business hours. Unscheduled interruptions may occur without notice.5.2 Directory Management & Connectivity

Due to performance considerations in certification suite, Surescripts recommends limiting the number of registrations per account to the directory. Surescripts reserves the right to remove directory registrations.

File Loads

Staging is intended for testing only. It is not intended for large file load testing. Generally the file size should not exceed 20 MB. Testing of larger formulary files may be allowed if coordinated with Integration.

Transaction Processing

Restriction on PHI

Staging is intended for testing only. PHI data is not allowed. Customers may only send transactions to Surescripts test accounts. Customers may not send transactions to other connected partners.

Volume Limitations

Due to staging environment limitations, production volume of transactions is NOT supported. Surescripts does not support performance or load testing in staging environment. The throughput of transactions should be limited to no more than 100 transactions per hour.

Overview

This Staging Environment Operations Guide (hereafter referred to as "this document") is provided by Surescripts to customers to provide a framework to outline responsibilities, expectations, and operating principles regarding customers' interconnection to pilot, early adopter and generally available services on the Surescripts® staging environment. The staging environment is primarily intended for developing to Surescripts’ specifications and testing functionality before advancing to production. The environment may also be used for user acceptance testing and product demos. The data in the staging environment is not guaranteed to represent life-like patients, prescribers, or pharmacies. This document defines availability and quality of service associated with the operations of the Surescripts staging environment for Surescripts and connected network customers. Although this document will establish the initial framework for staging environment operations, it will change over time as required and Surescripts shall provide email or bulletin notice to customers of any changes. This document is complementary to any service level commitments established within the appropriate agreements currently in place between the parties. In the event of any conflict between this document and any agreement between the parties, the agreement shall govern.

This document should be shared with customer business management, technology operations and support staff responsible for the deployment, development and day-to-day operations of Surescripts offered services specifically stated in this document. The information in this document is limited to Surescripts products and services active in staging.

Staging Environment Operations Guide Objectives

This document seeks to establish:

  • A mechanism to formalize the Surescripts and customer operational relationship, including Surescripts and customer responsibilities;
  • Guidelines for using staging environment
  • Expectations for availability of Surescripts staging environment; and
  • Standard processes for incident reporting and escalation.

Customer Review and Acceptance

A copy of this document will be made available prior to customers’ access to the Surescripts staging environment. The customer will be deemed to have received this document once Surescripts has sent this document to any of the notice addresses (e-mail, facsimile, or physical address) provided in the customer’s contractual agreement(s) with Surescripts.

Each customer must review the content of this document with all relevant staff, including Management, Production Support, Technology and Operations teams as soon as reasonably possible, but no later than the customer entering into staging on the Surescripts network. The customer will provide and review this document on an ongoing basis with any new staff providing services relevant to the subject matter herein. The customer will provide a copy of this document to their Technical Support team.

If the customer has any questions regarding this document or the adherence process, they should contact their Surescripts Account Executive/Account Manager.

If customer exceeds the usage limits defined in this document, Surescripts may suspend customer access to the Surescripts staging environment until issue has been resolved to Surescripts reasonable satisfaction.

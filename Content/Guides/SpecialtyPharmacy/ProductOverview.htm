Product Overview

About Specialty Patient Enrollment

The Specialty Patient Enrollment product gives providers that prescribe specialty medications the ability to electronically populate and submit enrollment data from within the provider vendor system workflow. Once the user has completed the specialty enrollment form, Surescripts compiles and sends the enrollment data to the appropriate pharmacy for the enrollment of the patient. Enrollment forms can be based on a specific medication, a medical condition, or a medical specialty with a medication mapping to one or more of the enrollment forms. In addition, Surescripts will maintain a general enrollment form that can be used for any medication where a specific enrollment form has not been identified.

About This Guide

Surescripts created this document to provide pharmacies information on Specialty Patient Enrollment, which provides a structured, electronic format for prescriber-specialty pharmacy communication of enrollment data using real-time electronic solutions. The Surescripts Specialty Patient Enrollment for Pharmacies Implementation Guide was created to assist pharmacy customers responsible for developing a system interface for these Specialty Patient Enrollment messages.

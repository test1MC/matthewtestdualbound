Rx Routing Overview

About Prescription Routing

As the nation's e-prescription network® for more than 15 years, Surescripts offers e-prescribing to healthcare providers and pharmacies serving all care settings – ambulatory, acute, post-acute, long term, and specialty. Electronic exchange of prescription information between prescribers and pharmacies improves prescription accuracy and saves time. Pharmacists realize a reduction in pharmacy phone calls and faxes related to prescription renewal authorizations, as well as a reduced need for pharmacy staff to enter prescription data manually.

About This Guide

The Surescripts Prescription Routing Companion Guide was created to assist customers responsible for developing a system interface for these electronic prescribing messages.

The terms "message" and "transaction" are used interchangeably throughout this guide. "Transaction" is used when referencing language pulled directly from a standards organization document which uses the term "transaction".

Medication History Implementation Guide (This Guide)

This Surescripts Medication History Ambulatory Implementation Guide was created to assist Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBMs) ,Prescriber system vendors in developing and transferring messages needed to provide medication history to physicians in an ambulatory setting. This document represents XML and EDIFACT implementations.

The audience for this document includes PBMs for the creation of responses for Ambulatory, or Acute vendors, and for Prescriber system vendors for the creation of requests, and understanding of responses. This guide describes the Surescripts medication history transaction sets and provides other information needed for their implementation.

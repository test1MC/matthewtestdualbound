CONDITION/RESPONSE CODED

<Consent>

Patient Consent Indicator

M = Rx History Request

C = Rx History Response

Values:

Y = Consent given

N = No consent

Note: If Patient Consent = N, it is expected the Medication History transactions would not be sent.

P = Prescriber. Patient gave consent for prescriber to only receive the medication history this prescriber prescribed.

X = Parental/Guardian consent on behalf of a minor for prescriber to receive the medication history from any prescriber

Z = Parental/Guardian consent on behalf of a minor for prescriber to only receive the medication history this prescriber prescribed

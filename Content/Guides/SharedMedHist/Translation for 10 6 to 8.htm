Translation for 10.6 to 8.1 and 8.1 to 10.6

Please note that translation rules for fields indicated in the sections above as optional according to the NCPDP standard but “Not used by Surescripts” or “Not used for Medication History” are not listed below. Surescripts will pass through compliant values sent in many of these “not used” fields where allowed by applicable versions of the standard, but exceptions apply. Participants should not assume that all values sent in fields indicated as “not used” will be passed through to recipients.

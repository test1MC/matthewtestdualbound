About This Guide

Surescripts created this document to assist pharmacy benefit managers (PBMs) in implementing the Medication Management for Adherence product using the file exchange format and Secure File Transfer Protocol (SFTP). SFTP file exchange between the PBM/payer vendor and Surescripts is used to minimize the cost of the pilot to the PBM/payer vendor.  This process will be used for the duration of the PBM/payer vendor pilot.

The audience for this document includes applicable PBM/payers responsible for implementing Medication Management for Adherence.

Transition to Production

Once all Surescripts requirements have been completed and testing is successful, the customer is ready to move into production. Surescripts will configure the production connection and validate successful operations with the customer. Once production is activated and validated, the Surescripts Medication Management for Adherence product team will schedule a meeting for the customer and Surescripts to discuss the following:

  • Lessons Learned and/or Satisfaction Survey
  • Production support contacts (from both Surescripts and the customer)
  • Support process
  • Support hours

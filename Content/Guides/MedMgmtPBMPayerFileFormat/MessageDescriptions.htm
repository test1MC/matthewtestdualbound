Message Descriptions

The message types supported by the file import are Medication Adherence and Proportion of Days Covered.

Medication Adherence

An adherence message, as indicated by claim activity, occurs when a member is not taking the medication as frequently as prescribed.

Proportion of Days Covered (PDC)

The PDC calculation is based on the fill dates and days supply for each fill of a prescription. PDC is defined as the total numbers of days with possession of medication in a period of time.

A PDC ratio will be provided by the PBM/payer for cholesterol, diabetes and hypertension regardless if the PDC is below or above 80%.

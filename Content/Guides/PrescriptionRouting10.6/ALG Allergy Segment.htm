ALG-010

NO KNOWN ALLERGIES

<NoKnownAllergies>

a1

Indicates if the sender does not know of any specific allergies for this patient.

If the sender sends “Y” (Yes), no other fields are sent in this segment.

Values:

Y = Yes

N = No

ALG-020

SOURCE OF INFORMATION

<SourceOfInformation>

a1

Sender indicates where the sender received the allergy information if known.

The source of information is the ultimate source. The values are the patient or patient representative, or observed by the clinician or provider.

For values, see the NCPDP External Code List (8000).

ALG-040

ADVERSE EVENT TYPE

<AdverseEvent>

  

Composite describes the type of product and intolerance suffered by the patient. The type of product should be classified with respect to whether the adverse event occurs in relationship with a medication, food, or environmental or other product. The adverse event should also be classified more specifically as an allergy, non-allergy intolerance, or just adverse reaction if that level of detail is not known.

If No Known Allergies ALG-010 = N (No) then this field is required, meaning allergies are known and will be reported

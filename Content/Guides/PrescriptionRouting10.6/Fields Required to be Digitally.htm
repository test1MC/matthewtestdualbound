Fields Required to be Digitally Signed

The fields defined in the table below are used in the signature block. The Interim Final Ruling outlines the fields that are required to be signed in sections: 1306.05 (a), 1306.05 (b), 1306.05 (c) and 1311.120(b) (9).

Fillable messages (see EPCS Message Workflow on page 1) that are for controlled substances are required to either be signed or contain the digital signature indicator flag. Messages that are not fillable are not required to be signed. The sender of the message is required to sign all fields in the digital signature, but the data may not be contained within the body of the message itself. So, the sender is signing that there is no data present for a particular field within the body of the message. This does not mean that you will find empty tags for these items in the body of the message, just that no data exists. For data types and lengths, refer to the sections above or the XML schema. The prescribed medication is what is to be signed for fillable messages.

When a supervisor of a non-DEA registered clinician is involved in prescribing, they are the official signer and their information should be in the prescriber field. Only the individual listed as the prescriber may sign the message. This individual must be registered with Surescripts and have the Controlled Substance service level enabled in the Surescripts directory.

For Date/Time fields in the table below, only the Date portion of the field is to be signed.

OBS – Observation Segment

For pediatric patients - As electronic prescribing/EHR systems capture this information, it is recommended that the Observation Segment with current information for patient height and weight (and any other pertinent information) be sent on prescriber-initiated transactions for prescriptions. If the Observation Segment is sent, the measurement date is recommended to be sent.

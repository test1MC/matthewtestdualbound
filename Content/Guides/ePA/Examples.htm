Examples

PA Submitted by a Person Other than the Prescriber

In this example, the initiator of the prior authorization process is a staff member in the provider vendor’s organization who can be reached at a different phone number than the prescriber. To provide this additional contact information, the ePA standard enables the initiating person to provide their separate contact information in the Provider composite of the prior authorization message (see Provider Elements in the NCPDP SCRIPT Implementation Guide for more information). If the PBM/payer needs to call or fax the initiator during processing, it uses the contact information contained in this Provider composite rather than the phone or fax in the Prescriber segment.

The requested drug is Lovenox (Lovenox 40 MG Per 0.4 ML Prefilled Syringe; RxNorm SBD 854236; NDC 58016487201).

This example does not include the status messages, or any other part of the normal message flow.

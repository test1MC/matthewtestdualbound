About This Guide

CompletEPA® Implementation Guide

The Surescripts CompletEPA® Guide was created to assist PBM/payers and provider vendors in developing and transferring messages needed to provide a series of messages based on the NCPDP Script standard. Each message supports a particular step in the prior authorization process, which electronically supports the exchange of information necessary to facilitate the request for coverage of a specific prescription drug under a patient’s pharmacy benefit.

The audience for this document includes any customer responsible for developing a system interface for these electronic prior authorization messages.

The terms PBM/payer or processor, who acts on behalf of the PBM/payer, are referred to as PBM/payer throughout this guide.

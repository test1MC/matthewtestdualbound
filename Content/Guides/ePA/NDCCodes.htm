NDC Codes

Provider vendors should send the Representative NDC of the drug, if available, in the PAInitiationRequest. The NDC is not required by the NCPDP standard, but PBM/payers may do additional validation on this field. Sending the NDC can help ensure that the drug can be easily identified in the PBM/payer system.

The most up to date NDC available for the requested drug should be sent based upon the drug database being utilized.

PBM/payers should not expect that provider vendors are always populating the NDC in the PAInitiationRequest and should consider additional drug matching logic in cases when the NDC is not populated.

For compounds, the NDC cannot be sent. This will be corrected in a future version of the standard.

Verify Example (XML)

Surescripts uses a Verify message as a response for certain messages. The following is an example of what a Verify message may look like.

Verify (XML)

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>

<transport:Message xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance" xmlns:pa-structures="http://www.ncpdp.org/schema/pa-structures" xmlns:structures="http://www.ncpdp.org/schema/structures" xmlns:script="http://www.ncpdp.org/schema/script" xmlns:datatypes="http://www.ncpdp.org/schema/datatypes" xmlns:transport="http://www.ncpdp.org/schema/transport" xsi:schemaLocation="http://www.ncpdp.org/schema/transport transport.xsd" TransportVersion="v2013111" TransactionVersion="v2013111" PA-StructuresVersion="v2013111" TransactionDomain="SCRIPT" DatatypesVersion="v2013111" ECLVersion="v2013111" StructuresVersion="v2013111">

   <transport:Header>

      <transport:To Qualifier="D">1234567890123</transport:To>

      <transport:From Qualifier="PY">1234563</transport:From>

      <transport:MessageID>888333</transport:MessageID>

      <transport:RelatesToMessageID>123</transport:RelatesToMessageID>

      <transport:SentTime>2014-10-15T10:15:12.1Z</transport:SentTime>

      <transport:Security>

         <transport:Sender>

            <transport:TertiaryIdentification>ACCT_ID_INSERTED_BY_SS</transport:TertiaryIdentification>

         </transport:Sender>

         <transport:Receiver>

            <transport:TertiaryIdentification>ACCT_ID_INSERTED_BY_SS</transport:TertiaryIdentification>

         </transport:Receiver>

      </transport:Security>

      <transport:SenderSoftware>

         <transport:SenderSoftwareDeveloper>MDLITE</transport:SenderSoftwareDeveloper>

         <transport:SenderSoftwareProduct>443</transport:SenderSoftwareProduct>

         <transport:SenderSoftwareVersionRelease>2.1</transport:SenderSoftwareVersionRelease>

      </transport:SenderSoftware>

   </transport:Header>

   <transport:Body>

      <transport:Verify>

         <transport:VerifyStatus>

            <transport:Code>010</transport:Code>

         </transport:VerifyStatus>

      </transport:Verify>

   </transport:Body>

</transport:Message>

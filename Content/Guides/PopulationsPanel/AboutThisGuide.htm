Product Overview

About Medication History for Populations - Panel Management

The Surescripts Medication History for Populations - Panel Management service assists health systems, hospitals and risk-bearing groups to better manage the health of their patient populations.

About This Guide

The Surescripts Medication History for Populations - Panel ManagementSurescripts Medication History Panel Management Implementation Guide was created to assist customers in developing software used to deliver historical claim data directly from PBM/payers, and fill data from pharmacies, for customer–defined populations directly to Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs) and other healthcare entities as well as to third party vendor resellers for use in analysis.

The audience for this document includes any customer responsible for developing a system interface for these electronic messages.

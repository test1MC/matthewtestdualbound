Message Flow

Medication Management for Adherence provides providers with the electronic delivery of PBM/payer medication adherence messages and patient medication adherence profile. Through the provider vendor interface, the practitioner can view and respond to PBM/payer medication adherence messages and access the patient's adherence profile. The graphic below depicts the transaction flow between customers in this process.

Figure #1 - Surescripts Medication Management for Adherence Message Flow

  1. PBM/payer sends messages and PDC ratios to Surescripts destined for a provider and a specific patient. Messages and PDC are XML based, discrete and structured data elements.
  2. Surescripts processes messages and PDC ratios upon receiving, forwards urgent messages directly to the provider’s worklist.
  3. Non-urgent messages and PDC ratios are available during the patient visit.
  4. When the provider has submitted feedback, Surescripts closes out those messages. This ensures that the only messages requiring action are displayed.
  5. There are two ways Surescripts delivers messages into a provider’s environment:
    1. When the PBM/payer designates a provider, urgent messages will be delivered to the provider’s worklist.
    2. All messages and PDC ratios will be available for viewing within patient’s chart.
  6. Physicians can provide feedback for messages read and feedback is sent to Surescripts for processing.
  7. PBM/payer pulls feedback to complete the communication loop.

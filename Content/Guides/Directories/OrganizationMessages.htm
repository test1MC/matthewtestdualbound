  OrganizationID

n1..10

Surescripts assigns this unique identifier in response to the “AddOrganization” message and recommends the customer system store this ID for use in future UpdateOrganization messages.

The OrganizationID of a General Organization may be used on AddProviderLocation or UpdateProviderLocation messages to link the provider record to a General Organization. The General Organization may also be linked as a parent or child to another General Organization (See ParentOrganizationID).

The OrganizationID and OrganizationName associated to a record will publish in the download file and can be used to establish a facility or organization hierarchy-based relationship for variety of messaging/information needs, such as clinical direct messaging.

OrganizationName

an1..100

The name of the Pharmacy, Payer, or General Organization.

Organization name cannot be the same as the name of the city in the address and it cannot be only numeric.

  • Pharmacy and Payer are an1..35.

When part of a larger healthcare enterprise, Surescripts recommends including identification of the larger enterprise in the name.

  AddressLine1

an1..40

Physical street address of the organization’s physical location where the patient encounters occur. P.O. Box is not allowed. Clinic and building names should not be used in this field.

Must contain at least one whitespace character. Physical sub-section identifiers (e.g. Suite, Floor) should be placed on AddressLine2.

C

    Number

n1..10

Required if PrimaryTelephone element is present.

  • Phone number must be 10 digits.
  • Area code cannot be '555'.
  • Phone number may not consist of all sequential numbers (e.g.'1234567890' or '0987654321') or one repeated digit (e.g. '0000000000'; however, '6126666666' is acceptable).
  • Required unless Organization has clinical messaging service levels ONLY.
  • The character limit noted in the Data Type applies to each individual value in a repeatable field element.

Note: Applies to number fields (for communication).

an1..254

Used in clinical messaging to identify an endpoint (sender or receiver) when information is exchanged. The direct address is required when the CIMessage and CIEvent service level is present.

C

  Status

an

Required if Notes is present.

Values:

  • Normal - Default setting – No notes required.
  • Informational -Indicates that the pharmacy location:
  • May be (or has) closed.
  • Has a name change.
  • Has possibly moved to an alternate NCPDPID routing identifier.
  • Alert - Indicates that the pharmacy location has an issue and possibly cannot fill the prescription.

C

  Notes

an1..70

Used to clarify the Informational or Alert status.

ReplaceNCPDPID

an1..35

For use when a Pharmacy location must obtain a new NCPDPID. Upon creation of the new record, the old NCPDPID may be placed in this field for customers to map, as they are able, to their internal local directories.

Pharmacy organization are responsible for managing this field. Surescripts will not use this information for message routing.

Must be 7 digits, unique, and not already in directory.

NCPDPID is only populated for Pharmacy type.

In UpdateOrganization message, NCPDPID must match an NCPDPID value in the directory.

Service Level Descriptions

For purposes of directory download inclusion and Admin Console search visibility, the service level is required on the Provider (SPI) or Organization record.  In regards to the ability to send or receive the transaction, the service level may not be required on the record level, but at the Surescripts Data Provider level or using a specific URL set up. Specifics on service level functionality should be referenced in the specific Product Implementation or associated Companion User Guide.

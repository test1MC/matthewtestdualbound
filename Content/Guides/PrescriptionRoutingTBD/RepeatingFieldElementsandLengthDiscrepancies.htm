Repeating Field Elements and Length Discrepancies

If the content of a repeatable element is longer than the standard allows due to carrying this data from a different standard (i.e. X12 271 to EDIFACT/XML RXHREQ), the element must repeat in order to transmit the complete content. In the X12 reference, fields permit up to 80 characters, whereas in this standard, up to 35 characters are permitted, e.g. PBM Unique Member ID, CardholderID, PatientAccountNumber.

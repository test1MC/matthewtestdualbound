Prescription Routing Implementation Guide (This guide)

The audience for this document includes any customer responsible for developing a system interface for these electronic prescribing messages.

1.2.1 TERMS USED FOR LTC IN THIS GUIDE

LTC – Long Term Care is a variety of services which help meet both the medical and non-medical needs of people with a chronic illness or disability who cannot care for themselves for long periods of time.

Provider of the prescription order – in the context of the RxFill message, may refer to the physician, prescriber, an agent acting on behalf of the prescriber, or the system they use.

Dispensed – in the context of the RxFill message, indicates the medication is no longer in the possession of the pharmacy and has been handed, shipped, or delivered to the patient (or the patient’s caregiver/representative). If the medication is still located in the pharmacy, it is not yet ‘dispensed’.

Prescriber – for LTC this is the provider of the prescription order.

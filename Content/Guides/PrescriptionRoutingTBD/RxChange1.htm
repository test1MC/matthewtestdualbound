Rx Change

In this example, the pharmacy is requesting permission to change the original (new) prescription. The pharmacy is requesting to change to another brand. In this scenario the pharmacy and prescriber are directly connected (e.g. via dedicated line). Since the prescriber will probably not be able to answer the change request real-time, the change response will come back to the pharmacy sometime in the future.

In the EDIFACT examples, line breaks are used at the end of the segments for display purposes – live messages should not contain line breaks.

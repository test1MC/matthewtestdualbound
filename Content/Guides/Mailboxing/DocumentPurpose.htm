About This Guide

The Surescripts Mailboxing Supplement was created to assist prescriber or pharmacy vendors with the implementation of the Surescripts Mailboxing service to retrieve messages. This guide is a supplement to the Surescripts E-Prescribing Companion Guide and only lists the differences in using messaging for the Mailboxing service customers.

Patient Enrollment File LoadNotifications Overview

About Patient Enrollment File LoadNotifications

The healthcare landscape is shifting to value-based care and ACOs, pushing risk further toward IDNs and hospitals more than ever before. As a result effective care management is essential to improve patient care and reduce costs.

Prescriptions are a critical tool for treating chronically ill patients, who account for 91% of all prescriptions in the U.S. Access to timely dispensed medication data is a prerequisite for effective care management of patient populations and is currently not available on a near real-time basis today.

Patient Notifications addresses this problem by delivering near real-time notifications based on user-customized criteria directly into the customer’s workflow (e.g., cardiology patient’s statin prescription was not picked at a pharmacy). Surescripts will generate these notifications via the Patient Notifications Accelerator by continuously monitoring the patient’s medication behaviors for customer-selected notification criteria. Allowing Surescripts to generate notifications based on continuous data monitoring will result in more insight vs. making a care provider request information themselves.

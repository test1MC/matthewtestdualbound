Status

The Status message is sent synchronously from Surescripts to the acute/LTC in response to the RXHREQ and is sent synchronously from the acute/LTC to Surescripts in response to the RXHRES. Status message is an NCPDP message that indicates the acceptance of the RXHREQ and/or the RXHRES.

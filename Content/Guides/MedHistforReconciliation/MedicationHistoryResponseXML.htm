AdditionalTraceNumber

Since up to two RXHRES messages can be sent to the requester, the AdditionalTraceNumber field will contain either "1" or "2", indicating whether it is the first RXHRES or the second RXHRES that is being returned. It will be populated with "1" even if there is only 1 RXHRES being returned.

TertiaryIdentifier

Populated with the value NTR (Non-targeted Request) to indicate it is the version of MHR that does not require a 270/271 eligibility request.

ApprovalReasonCode

AQ (More History Available) is sent to indicate that another RXHRES will be returned.

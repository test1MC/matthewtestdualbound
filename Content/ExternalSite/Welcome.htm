Surescripts Online Documentation

Welcome to the Surescripts Online Documentation Site

This site holds implementation guide documentation or supporting information published by the Surescripts Standards team.

The Standards team is here to:

  • Provide clear requirements to upcoming projects as they relate to current / future standards.
  • Provide leadership and collaboration with industry standards organizations.
  • Provide expertise to staff and to customers.
  • Create and maintain Implementation Guides, Companion Guides and other documentation.

 

 

 

 

Standards Guide Types

  • Surescripts Implementation Guide – Provides the information needed to successfully implement and pass certification/complete message validation on a product or service. When applicable Surescripts product/service schemas will be provided with the guide.
  • Surescripts Companion Guide - Relates to a base standard. The purpose of a companion guide is to offer specific field element instructions, additional explanations, and suggested workflow information. Companion guides do not reproduce the standard in its entirety.
  • Surescripts Supplement Guide – Contains information on a service or product feature that is an addition to an existing base product/service.
  • Surescripts User Guide – Provides a basic overview of an application and the necessary foundation for end users to use the application.

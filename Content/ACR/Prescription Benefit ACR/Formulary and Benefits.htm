Formulary and Benefits

General Formulary and Benefits Requirements

4.1 The participant shall implement and maintain a comprehensive, industry-accepted drug information source that is mappable to NDC drug codes and updated at least monthly. Acceptable data sources include the following:

  • Drug Information Technologies (DIT)
  • First Databank
  • Gold Standard Drug Database
  • Lexi-Comp
  • Multum
  • Medispan
  • Thomson Reuters Micromedex/RED Book

4.2 The participant shall implement within its application updated formulary information published by PBMs within one week of its availability.

4.3 The application shall identify, based on the drug file it utilizes, if a drug is:

  1. brand or generic
  2. over-the-counter (OTC) or prescription-only
  3. compound
  4. supply

4.17 Third party sources of formulary information unaffiliated with Surescripts-affiliated formulary publishers should be clearly labeled as such.

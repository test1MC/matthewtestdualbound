Receiving Messages

5.1 The participant’s application shall make the message available to the intended recipient(s) within one minute of receiving it.

5.2 The participant’s application shall be able to receive a message of at least 20 MB.

5.3 The participant’s application shall be able to display, at a minimum, the following from the message.

  1. Date/Time
  2. Sender (From)
  3. Subject
  4. Message Body (text/plain or text/html part)

5.4 The participant’s application shall be able to display, at a minimum, the following attachment types: HTML, RTF, PDF, TXT and CCD.

5.5 The participant’s application shall, at a minimum, make all attachments regardless of type available to download unless the attachment type is filtered for security reasons.

5.6 If an attachment is filtered for security reasons, the participant’s application shall notify the recipient of the message the attachment was removed and the filename of the attachment.

Phone Number

PVD-090-01 Communication Number <Number>

PTT-070-01 Communication Number <Number>

Phone Number shall*106R-104 be in the format 5552223333X4444 - where 555 is the area code, 2223333 is the main number and X4444 is the extension. Shall*106R-105 not be populated with all zeros or other default values. (Please note this extension is just an example. Less/more than four digits is allowed).

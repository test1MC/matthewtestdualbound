Introduction

Document Purpose

This document presents Net2Net Direct XDR Basic Messaging Certification Requirements for Surescripts participants. The audience for this document includes the participant’s development, business analysis, product management and implementation staff and representatives of the Surescripts implementation, certification and compliance staff.

The Surescripts Net2Net Direct XDR Basic Messaging Certification Requirements are a set of additional requirements above those mandated in the Surescripts Net2Net Direct XDR Implementation Guide.

All of the certification requirements must be met in order for a participant to access the Surescripts production network.

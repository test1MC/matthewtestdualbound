Overview

Document Purpose

This document presents Application Certification Requirements for Surescripts participants. The audience for this document includes the participant’s development, business analyst, product managers and implementation staff, and representatives of the Surescripts’ implementation and certification staff.

The Application Certification Requirements are a set of additional requirements above those mandated in the Surescripts NCPDP Medication History Acute Implementation Guide.

All of the Application Certification Requirements must be met in order for a participant to access the Surescripts production network.
